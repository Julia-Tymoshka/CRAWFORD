Welcome to Play Park

The indoor play offer at Crawfords Play park is unique and links closely to the heritage of the farm with subtle theming, while maintaining maximum play value for the children using the play space. Crawford Farm Park has enhanced its family picnic area with the addition of an exciting new farm themed playground designed and installed by Russell Play. Alongside the picnic tables children can now play at being a farmer, a vet or a farm shop owner while other family members may choose to relax with a drink from Crawford Cafe.
