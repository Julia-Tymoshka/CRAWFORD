Welcome to Crawford

Take a break from city life for a day at the beautiful Crawford Farm Park and Café. It’s nearby Perth but it has all the wonders of the countryside - from PYO fruit, to farm animals, beautiful woodland walks.

Visit To Crawford

Whatever the weather there is always something to do at the Crawford Farm Park!
We have plenty of exciting activities – Captain your own boat with our amazing bumper boats, soak your family and friends in water wars, we even have a playpark, giant sand pit, trampolines, ringo sledging, a giant aerial runway and jumping pillows to keep you amused! Quad biking is always great fun and we have quads for all ages – small electric quads which adults and children can go on together, kid’s quad bikes for children aged 7 -11 and adult quads for visitors who are over the age of 12 and over 5 feet tall. Our undercover Play 'n’ Wild adventure barn has three giant drop slides and a two storey soft play area suitable for all ages – We also have 'Crawford Farm Caffe' in the play barn offering delicious coffee, sandwiches and cakes and restaurant selling a variety of hot and cold food and drink. As well as all our fun activities we have many different animals for you to meet and learn all about - visit our Little Critters Animal Barn to see lots of our smaller animals such as rabbits, guinea pigs, degus, mice and birds or venture outside into our animal zone and see many of our larger residents including Ralph the Camel, Troy the Tapir, llamas, ponies, donkeys and goats. The Park is also home to various breeds of monkeys, lemurs and meerkats!

Crawford Farm Caffe

Visit Crawford Farm Caffe in the summer to enjoy fruit picking season. Hunting for the perfect strawberry is a kids’ favourite, plus you can pick your own mix of berries and make tasty jam for the winter. Fancy a bit of detective work? Set on one of Crawford Nature Detective Trails with the kids and see where the young Sherlocks will take you.

Crawford Family Zoo

One of the kids’ favourite things to do is meeting the animals. Say “Hello” to the little ponies, the chickens and everyone’s favourite piglets. The farm cafe has a safe outdoor play area, featuring slides, swings, and tractor rides. There you can spot the playful Pygmy goats.
